We built ai.market because we believe data should be as easy to find and buy as software.

## The Problem

Today, acquiring training data, specialized datasets, or AI model access for enterprise use is a fragmented, manual process. Buyers spend weeks negotiating access. Sellers have no efficient distribution channel. And the data itself - the most valuable asset - often has to leave the seller's infrastructure just to be evaluated.

## Our Approach

ai.market takes a different approach. We connect buyers and sellers without ever touching the data itself.

**Sellers** install our open-source tools (AIM Channel for datasets, AIM Node for models and pipelines) on their own infrastructure. An embedded AI assistant profiles the asset locally, generating metadata, quality scores, and descriptions. Only the metadata is published to the marketplace.

**Buyers** search, evaluate, and purchase through ai.market. When they commit, the delivery happens peer-to-peer - encrypted, direct, and auditable.

## What's Next

We're actively onboarding sellers in biotech, financial services, and industrial IoT. If you have data or AI capabilities to monetize, [get in touch](/support).
